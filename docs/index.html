Finance Sentiment Analysis

Introduction

The project examines trends in the stock market during April 2024 by analyzing CNBC headline articles, focusing on the frequency of key terms like "inflation." It aims to answer the following questions:

-Trend Analysis

-Sentiment Analysis

-Event Correlation

Big Picture Conclusions

Based on preliminary findings, the frequency of the word "inflation" in CNBC headlines correlates with significant stock market movements. During periods of increased headline activity mentioning "inflation," the stock market exhibits higher volatility. This suggests that inflation-related news has a direct impact on investor sentiment and market behavior.
